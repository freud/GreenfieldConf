We’re always on the lookout for talented people who believe they can contribute not only to the success of our business but also to our culture and values. As a company with ambitions for global expansion, we currently have offices in the UK, Germany and Poland, with additional opportunities across Europe.

MetaPack is a technology company with functions including software engineering, QA and analysis. Other departments cover carrier management, marketing, finance, sales, presales and implementation, project management, product management, customer and client support, professional services, HR, legal and compliance and IT.

If you’re interested in working with us but don’t see an opening listed, please don’t hesitate to get in touch. We will review any application and respond accordingly, and are happy to answer questions. We like to support talented people at the early stages of their career, so apart from permanent positions we also offer internships and work experience opportunities.
